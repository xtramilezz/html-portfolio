The Best Movies According to Buji

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

This is my favorite historical epic and especially the music in this movie is so inspiring.

The Shawshank Redemption

It's one of the best classical American movies of all-time. It's admiring to me, how someone goes so quiet when things are at the worst possible but keeps going forward with hope only until the very end.

Jurassic World 2015

It's an awesome sci-fi, thriller movie about deadly dinosaur gets loose at the newest theme park Jurassic Island.
